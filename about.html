The Secret of Our Success!

Our community has strong leaders and committed citizens. A small number of committed people can make a big difference in a community. Leadership is critical, but often unappreciated. Every community has naysayers. Whatever the civic or community leaders propose to do, some people will always say things like: “you can’t do it”, “it won’t work”, “it costs too much”, “we tried that already”. And, “no”, is a very powerful word in a small community, but leaders of successful communities know that “yes” is a more powerful word. Yes, we can make this community a better place to live in, to look at, to work in, to visit.
